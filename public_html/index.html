Alphabet Song Game™

Helpful Information

The Alphabet Song Game™ is a simple, fun way to teach the alphabet.  Although easy to play, the 22 lessons go well beyond recognizing letter shapes.  Students learn to:

  • recognize upper and lowercase letter shapes.
  • recognize letter names.
  • distinguish each letter among similarly shaped letters.
  • distinguish commonly confused letter pairs.
  • distinguish uppercase and lowercase letters.

The Alphabet Song Game™ is designed to allow students to work independently, but when working with a young child, we strongly suggest working alongside the child—at least through the first two lessons.

The automatic lesson selection built in to the app will give students who score 90% correct or higher the opportunity to repeat the lesson they just completed or move on to the next lesson.  When students score below 90%, they are only offered the option to repeat the lesson just completed.
